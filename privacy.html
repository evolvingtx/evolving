Introduction

Evolving Therapy & Wellness ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website evolving-therapy-wellness.netlify.app and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our site.

Information We Collect

Contact Form Submissions

When you submit the contact form on our website, we collect your name, email address, and message. This information is processed by Netlify Forms and stored securely.

Payment Information

Digital product purchases are processed through PayPal. We do not store credit card information directly. PayPal's Privacy Policy governs the security of payment data.

Website Usage Data

We may collect information about how you interact with our site, such as pages visited and time spent. This helps us improve user experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and requests
  • To process product purchases and send digital downloads
  • To send you updates about new resources or services (only if you opt in)
  • To improve and optimize our website
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. If you have concerns, please contact us at admin@evolve-OT.com.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Netlify: Website hosting and form processing
  • PayPal: Payment processing for digital products
  • Google Fonts: Font delivery

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Important: This Site Is Not HIPAA-Compliant

Evolving Therapy & Wellness website is not HIPAA-compliant and should not be used to submit, store, or transmit protected health information (PHI). Clinical therapy services and patient records are managed through our secure EMR system (Fusion/Ensora), not this website.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at admin@evolve-OT.com.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@evolve-OT.com.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
